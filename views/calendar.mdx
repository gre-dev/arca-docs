<Info>
  Calendar view is available on the **Starter** and **Business** plans.
</Info>

Calendar view shows tasks plotted on a calendar based on their due dates and start dates. It's the fastest way to see what's coming up, identify days that are overloaded, and reschedule work before issues arise.

## Switch between layouts

Toggle between **month** and **week** modes:

- **Month:** a bird's-eye view of the full period, great for planning and deadline awareness
- **Week:** a focused view of the days immediately ahead, great for daily planning

## Reschedule by dragging

Drag a task to a new date on the calendar to update its due date instantly, without opening the task.

## Click to view full details

Click any task on the calendar to open its full detail view and make edits.

## Get the most from calendar view

- **Assign due dates to all tasks:** tasks without dates won't appear on the calendar.
- **Spot overloaded days early:** if too many tasks land on the same day, drag some to adjoining days during planning.
- **Filter by person:** add an assignee filter to review one teammate's upcoming deadlines or check your own.

<Tip>
  Create a saved calendar view filtered to your name; it becomes your personal
  planning dashboard for the week.
</Tip>
