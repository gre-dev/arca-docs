Arca is a task management app built for teams who want clarity without complexity. It helps you capture work, assign ownership, track progress, and collaborate, all in one place, without scattered to-do lists or missed commitments.

## Why teams use Arca

- **One place for all your work:** tasks, calendar, and conversations live together, so nothing gets lost across chat, email, and spreadsheets
- **Clear ownership:** every task has an assignee, so there's never a question of who is responsible
- **Real-time collaboration:** comments, mentions, and notifications keep your team aligned without endless status meetings
- **Flexible views:** see your work as a list, board, calendar, or timeline based on what you're planning
- **Progress at a glance:** dashboards and insights show how work is progressing across your whole workspace
