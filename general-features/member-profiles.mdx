Member profiles open as a drawer on the right side of the screen, giving you a quick look at a teammate without navigating away from what you're doing.

<Note>
  Arca never shows a member's email address in their profile. This is intentional — workspaces are sometimes shared with external clients, and you shouldn't have to expose your internal team's contact details just because they're collaborating in the same workspace.
</Note>

## How to open a profile

You can open a member's profile from multiple places:

- **Task detail page** — click the name of the task's creator in the task sidebar
- **Comments and activity** — click any member's avatar in the comments or activity feed
- **@mentions** — click a @mention inside a comment to open that person's profile

## What's shown

| Field | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Name** | The member's display name |
| **Role** | Their role in the workspace: Owner, Admin, Member, or Viewer |
| **Online status** | A live indicator showing whether the member is currently active in the workspace |
| **Total points** | Points accumulated from completing tasks on time or early |
| **Joined** | The month and year they joined the workspace |
| **Working on** | A list of tasks currently assigned to them that are not yet completed, each shown with its status icon |

The **Working on** section is only shown if the member has active tasks assigned to them.
