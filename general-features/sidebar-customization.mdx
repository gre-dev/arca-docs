The sidebar is your navigation hub. Arca lets you customize it so you only see what you actually use, keeping it clean, short, and fast to navigate.

## Customize the sidebar

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Go to **Settings > Preferences** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Customize sidebar">
    Find the **Customize sidebar** option and click it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Toggle items on or off">
    Enable or disable the sections and items you want to show or hide.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can also access sidebar customization by right-clicking any whitespace in
  the sidebar and choosing **Customize sidebar** from the context menu.
</Tip>

## Personal layout

Sidebar customization is per-person; changes you make only affect your own sidebar. Your teammates keep their own layouts.

## What you can toggle

The following sidebar items can be shown or hidden individually:

- **Inbox**: your notification centre
- **Planned**: all dated tasks grouped by due date
- **Views**: your saved workspace views
- **Dashboards**: charts and analytics (visible to admins and owners only)

<Tip>
  Hide rarely-used items to keep your sidebar short and scannable. You can
  always re-enable them from Preferences at any time.
</Tip>
