Search lets you jump directly to any task in your workspace without scrolling through lists or remembering where something lives. It's the fastest way to find work when you need it.

## Open search

- Press **Cmd+K** on macOS or **Ctrl+K** on Windows and Linux.
- Or click the search icon in the sidebar.

## What search covers

- Task titles and descriptions
- Assignee names
- List and folder names

## Search tips

- Type a few words from the task title; you don't need the full title.
- Search spans all lists and folders in your workspace, so you don't need to know where a task is before finding it.
- Press **Enter** to open the first result, or use the arrow keys to navigate and select.

<Tip>
  Use **Cmd+K** / **Ctrl+K** as your go-to shortcut any time you need to find
  something quickly. It's faster than clicking through folders and lists.
</Tip>
