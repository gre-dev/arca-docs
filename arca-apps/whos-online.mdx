Who's Online shows a real-time green presence indicator on member avatars throughout Arca. When a teammate is active in the workspace, their avatar shows a green dot. This makes it easy to know who is available before you assign a task, @mention someone in a comment, or start a conversation.

<Note>Who's Online is always on and cannot be disabled.</Note>

---

## Where you see online status

Presence indicators appear in several places across the workspace:

- **Task assignee picker**: see who is online when assigning a task or creating a new one.
- **Settings → Members**: the full member list shows a green dot next to anyone who is currently active.
- **Timeline view**: member avatars in the assignee column show live presence.
