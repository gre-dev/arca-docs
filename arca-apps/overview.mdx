title: "Overview"
description: "Optional workspace features you can enable or disable. Each app adds a specific capability to your workspace."
icon: "grid-2"
---

Arca Apps is a catalog of optional features that you can turn on or off per workspace. Enabling an app makes it available to all workspace members immediately. Disabling it removes it from the interface without deleting any data.

Go to **Settings → Arca Apps** to open the catalog.

<Note>
  Only workspace **Owners** and **Admins** can enable or disable apps. Some apps
  are always on and cannot be toggled, their card shows an "Always on" label.
</Note>
