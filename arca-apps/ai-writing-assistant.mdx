The AI Writing Assistant adds AI-powered text actions directly into the task description editor. Select any text, click the sparkle icon, and choose how you want to rewrite it like more professional, friendlier, more concise, or as a plain summary.

<Note>The AI Writing Assistant is always on and cannot be disabled.</Note>
