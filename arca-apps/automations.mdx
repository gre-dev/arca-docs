---
title: "Advanced Automations"
description: "Create rules that automatically act on tasks when specific events occur, no manual work required."
icon: "bolt"
---

Advanced Automations lets you define trigger-condition-action rules for your workspace. When a task event happens (like a status change or a due date passing), Arca evaluates your conditions and runs the configured actions like updating fields, sending notifications, assigning members, and more.

<Note>Advanced Automations is always on and cannot be disabled.</Note>
