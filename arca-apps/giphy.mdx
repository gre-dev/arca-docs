Giphy Support adds a GIF picker to the comment editor on every task. Search Giphy's library, click a GIF, and it appears inline in your comment. Perfect for quick reactions, celebrations, or just keeping things fun.

---

## Enable Giphy Support

Giphy Support is **off by default**. A workspace Owner or Admin must enable it first.

Go to **Settings → Arca Apps**, find **Giphy Support**, and click its toggle. The GIF button appears in the comment editor for all workspace members immediately.

---

## Insert a GIF in a comment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a task">Click any task to open it.</Step>
  <Step title="Click in the comment editor">
    Scroll to the comment box at the bottom of the task.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the GIF picker">
    Click the **GIF** button in the comment toolbar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search or browse">
    Type a keyword to search Giphy, or scroll through the trending GIFs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Insert the GIF">
    Click any GIF to insert it into your comment. Submit the comment to share it
    with your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>
