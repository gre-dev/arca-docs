---
title: "Comment Reactions"
description: "React to any comment with an emoji instead of writing a reply. Great for quick acknowledgements and feedback."
icon: "face-smile"
---

Comment Reactions lets you add emoji reactions to comments on any task. Click the emoji button on a comment, pick an emoji, and your reaction appears under the comment. Each person can add one of each emoji per comment. The comment's author gets a notification when someone reacts.

---

## Enable Comment Reactions

Comment Reactions is **on by default**. A workspace Owner or Admin can disable it from **Settings → Arca Apps** if the workspace does not want it.

---

## React to a comment

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a task">Click any task to open it.</Step>
  <Step title="Click the emoji button">
    Click the smiley face icon to open the emoji picker.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick an emoji">
    Click any emoji. Your reaction appears below the comment with a count.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Remove a reaction

Click your existing reaction badge below a comment to remove it.

---

## Notifications

When someone reacts to your comment, you receive a notification in your **Inbox**. The notification shows who reacted and which task the comment belongs to.
