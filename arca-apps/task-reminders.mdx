---
title: "Task Reminders"
description: "Set personal reminders on any task and get a notification at exactly the time you choose."
icon: "alarm-clock"
---

Task Reminders lets you schedule one or more personal notifications on any task. You choose when you want to be reminded, whether that's in 5 minutes or 30 days, and Arca sends you a notification at that time. Reminders are personal; only you see the ones you set on a task.

<Note>Task Reminders is always on and cannot be disabled.</Note>

---

## Set a reminder

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a task">
    Click any task to open it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the task menu">
    Click the **⋯** menu in the task header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a reminder time">
    Hover over **Remind me** to see the available intervals. Click any option to activate it:

    | Option | Time before reminder |
    |---|---|
    | In 5 minutes | 5 minutes from now |
    | In 15 minutes | 15 minutes from now |
    | In 30 minutes | 30 minutes from now |
    | In 1 hour | 1 hour from now |
    | In 2 hours | 2 hours from now |
    | In 3 hours | 3 hours from now |
    | Tomorrow | 24 hours from now |
    | In 7 days | 7 days from now |
    | In 30 days | 30 days from now |

    A checkmark appears next to any active reminder.

  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Set multiple reminders

You can activate more than one interval at the same time. For example, you can set both an "In 1 hour" and a "Tomorrow" reminder on the same task. Each one fires independently at the scheduled time.

---

## Remove a reminder

Open the **⋯** menu on the task, hover over **Remind me**, and click any checked interval to deactivate it. The checkmark disappears and the reminder is cancelled.

---

## How reminders are delivered

When a reminder fires, you receive a notification in your **Inbox**. If you have desktop notifications enabled, a desktop alert appears at the same time. Same goes for mobile push notifications.
