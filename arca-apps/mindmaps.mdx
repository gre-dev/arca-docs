Mindmaps gives you a full visual canvas for building mind maps, flowcharts, and diagrams. Add shapes and sticky notes, connect them with styled edges, upload images, and group related areas with section containers. Every canvas auto-saves as you work.

## Enable Mindmaps

Mindmaps is **off by default**. A workspace Owner or Admin must enable it first.

Go to **Settings → Arca Apps**, find **Mindmaps**, and click its toggle. Once enabled, a **Mindmaps** entry appears in the left sidebar for all workspace members.
