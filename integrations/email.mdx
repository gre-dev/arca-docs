title: "Email"
description: "Create tasks by forwarding emails to your workspace's unique email address."

---

The Email integration gives your workspace a unique inbound email address. Forward any email to that address and Arca automatically creates a task from it. The subject becomes the title, the body becomes the description, and attachments are added to the task.

## Requirements

You must be an **Admin** or **Owner** of the Arca workspace to enable and configure the Email integration.

---

## Enable the integration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integrations settings">
    In Arca, click your workspace name in the top-left, then go to **Settings →
    Integrations**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Email integration">
    Find the **Email** card and click it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a default list">
    Use the **Default list** dropdown to choose the list where email-created
    tasks will be placed. A list is required before the integration can be
    enabled.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a default priority (optional)">
    Use the **Default priority** dropdown to assign a priority to every task
    created via email. If you leave this blank, tasks are created with no
    priority.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable">
    Click **Enable Email Integration**. Arca generates a unique email address
    for your workspace and displays it on the settings page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Your workspace email address

After enabling the integration, a unique address in the format `arca+<token>@intake.getarca.app` is shown on the settings page. Copy it with the copy button next to the field.

Any email sent to this address creates a task in the configured list:

| Email field               | Mapped to                                      |
| ------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| Subject                   | Task title                                     |
| Body (plain text or HTML) | Task description                               |
| Attachments               | Task attachments (up to 25 MB total per email) |

<Note>
  The email address is unique to your workspace and secret, anyone who has it
  can create tasks. Keep it private or reset it if it is ever shared
  accidentally.
</Note>

---

## Update settings

You can change the default list and default priority at any time without resetting your email address:

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations → Email**.
2. Update the **Default list** or **Default priority** fields.
3. Click **Save Settings**.

---

## Reset the email address

Resetting generates a new email address and immediately invalidates the old one. Any forwarding rules or contacts using the old address will stop working.

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations → Email**.
2. Scroll to the **Reset Email Address** section and click **Reset**.
3. Confirm the action.

Arca generates a new address and displays it on the settings page.

<Warning>
  The old address stops working immediately after a reset. Update any email
  clients or forwarding rules that use the previous address.
</Warning>

---

## Disconnect the integration

Disconnecting removes the email address permanently. Any emails sent to the old address will no longer create tasks.

1. Go to **Settings → Integrations → Email**.
2. Click **Disconnect** in the actions bar.
3. Confirm the action.

You can re-enable the integration at any time to get a new address, but the previous address cannot be restored.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I forwarded an email but no task was created">
    Check that the integration is still enabled by going to **Settings →
    Integrations → Email**. If it shows as connected, verify that you forwarded
    the email to the exact address shown on the settings page, including the
    `arca+` prefix. Addresses are case-insensitive; extra spaces or characters
    in the address will cause delivery to fail.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Attachments are missing from the task">
    The Email integration supports attachments up to 25 MB total per email. If
    the combined size of all attachments exceeds this limit, attachments may be
    skipped. Try resending with smaller or fewer files.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I want to send emails from a different email address">
    The integration accepts email from any sender, there is no allowlist. As
    long as the email is delivered to the correct workspace address, a task will
    be created regardless of who sent it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My email address was shared accidentally">
    Reset the email address immediately from **Settings → Integrations → Email →
    Reset**. The old address becomes invalid at once and a new one is generated.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
