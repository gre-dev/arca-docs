Arca integrates with third-party services so you can bring your workflows together in one place. Integrations are configured at the workspace level, meaning a single connection covers all members of that workspace.

## Managing integrations

Go to **Settings → Integrations** in your Arca workspace to connect, configure, or disconnect any integration. You must be an **Admin** or **Owner** to manage integrations.

Each integration card shows a checkmark when it is active, giving you a quick overview of what is connected.
