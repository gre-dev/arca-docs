Arca integrates with third-party services so you can bring your workflows together in one place. Some integrations are workspace-level (one connection shared by all members), while others are personal (each team member connects their own account).

## Managing integrations

Go to **Settings → Integrations** in your Arca workspace to connect, configure, or disconnect any integration. Workspace-level integrations require an **Admin** or **Owner** role to manage. Personal integrations can be connected by any workspace member.

Each integration card shows a checkmark when it is active, giving you a quick overview of what is connected.
