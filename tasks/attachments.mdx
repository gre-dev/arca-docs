Keeping files attached to tasks means your team doesn't have to dig through email threads, shared drives, or chat messages to find what they need. Everything relevant to a task lives with the task.

## Add an attachment

Open a task and click the **paperclip icon** in the task sidebar. Select one or more files from your computer to upload.

## Download or remove attachments

- **Supported file formats** (images, PDFs, and similar) open in a preview popup when clicked. From there, use the **Download** button to save the file.
- **Unsupported formats** are downloaded directly when clicked.
- Use the delete action to remove an attachment you no longer need.

## What to attach

- **Design files:** mockups, specs, or screenshots for a visual or UI task
- **Reference documents:** briefs, research, or requirements that give context to the assignee
- **Bug evidence:** error logs, screen recordings, or reproduction steps for a bug report
- **Deliverables:** completed work like reports, exports, or final assets submitted for review

<Note>
  Attachments are accessible to all workspace members who can view the task.
  Avoid uploading files that shouldn't be shared with the full workspace.
</Note>
