Your profile shows your name and avatar throughout the app: in task assignments, comments, and the sidebar.

## Update your profile

1. At the bottom of the sidebar, click your name or avatar.
2. A modal will open where you can update your **display name** and **profile picture**.
3. Save your changes.

## Why your profile matters

- Your name appears in task assignments and comments.
- Your avatar is shown in the sidebar and activity streams.
- Keeping your profile up to date helps teammates recognize you quickly.
