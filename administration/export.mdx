Exporting creates a snapshot of your workspace data in a standard CSV format that you can open in a spreadsheet, share with stakeholders, or import into another tool.

## Export your workspace

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Go to **Settings > Import / export** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the export option">
    Click the export button to generate the CSV file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download the file">
    Download the CSV to your computer.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## When to export

- **Regular backups:** keep a periodic snapshot of your workspace state
- **Reporting:** pull task data into a spreadsheet for custom analysis or stakeholder reports
- **Migration:** move your work to another tool or archive a completed project
- **Before deleting:** always export before deleting a workspace or list

<Tip>
  Export before making large structural changes to your workspace, so you have a
  fallback if anything goes wrong.
</Tip>
