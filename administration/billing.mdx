Billing is managed per workspace. Only workspace owners can change the plan or view invoices.

## View your current plan

Go to **Settings > Billing** to see:

- Your current plan (Free, Starter, or Business)
- Next billing date and status
- Recent invoices

## Upgrade or change plans

Click **Compare plans** to see what features each tier includes. When you choose a new plan, you’ll be directed to a secure checkout to complete the purchase.

## Invoices

Your recent invoices are available on the Billing page. You can download them for accounting or expense reports.

<Tip>If you need support with billing, reach out to your account administrator or the Arca support team.</Tip>
