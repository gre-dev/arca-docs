Billing is managed per workspace. Only the workspace **owner** can access the Billing page. Admins and other roles see an "Access Restricted" message.

## View your current plan

Go to **Settings > Billing** to see your current plan card, which shows:

- Your plan name (Free, Starter, or Business)
- Number of seats in your workspace
- Total cost based on seat count and billing interval (monthly or yearly)

## Upgrade or change plans

Click **See other plans** (Free plan) or **Compare all plans** (paid plan) to open the plan comparison. When you choose a new plan, a secure Stripe checkout opens in your browser to complete the purchase.

## Manage an active subscription

If you are on a paid plan, a **Manage subscription** button appears on the plan card. Clicking it opens the Stripe billing portal in your browser, where you can change your plan, update payment details, or cancel your subscription.

If a cancellation is already scheduled, the button changes to **Resume subscription** so you can reverse it before the period ends.

## Invoices

Your recent invoices appear at the bottom of the Billing page, showing the invoice number, date, amount, and payment status. Click **Download PDF** on any invoice to save it for accounting or expense reports.
