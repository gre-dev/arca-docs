Workspaces have members with different roles that control access and permissions.

## Roles

- **Owner**: Full control over the workspace, including billing and member management.
- **Admin**: Can manage workspace settings, members, and content.
- **Member**: Can create and edit tasks, but may have limited settings access.
- **Viewer**: Can view tasks and boards but cannot make changes.

## Add members

1. Open **Settings > Members**.
2. Click **Invite member**.
3. Enter their email and choose a role.
4. The invitee will receive an email with a link to join.

## Change roles

From the member list, select the role dropdown to update permissions.

<Tip>Use the Viewer role for stakeholders who need visibility but shouldn’t edit tasks.</Tip>
