Insights are pre-built charts you can add to a dashboard. They answer the most common questions about your team's work without needing to configure custom reports from scratch.

## How to access insights

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Dashboards">Click **Dashboards** in the sidebar.</Step>
  <Step title="Open or create a dashboard">
    Select an existing dashboard or create a new one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a chart">
    Click **Add chart** to see the available insight types.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Available insights

- **Tasks completed over time:** track velocity and see if it's trending up or down
- **Tasks by status:** understand how your backlog is distributed (how much is in progress vs. blocked?)
- **Tasks by priority:** are you spending time on high-priority work or getting pulled into low-priority tasks?
- **Tasks per assignee:** identify workload imbalances across the team
- **Burndown:** see if you're on track to complete work by a target deadline

## Use insights in reviews

Bring insights into team meetings to turn raw data into decisions:

- **Retrospectives:** use the velocity chart to evaluate how the sprint went
- **Weekly planning:** use the assignee chart to spot if one person is carrying too much
- **Check-ins:** use the status chart to see where work is getting stuck

<Tip>
  Filter insights by date range or list to narrow the data to a specific project
  or sprint period.
</Tip>
